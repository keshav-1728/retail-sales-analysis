# Retail Sales Analysis
### SQL + Python | 10,000 transactions | Business Intelligence

---

## The Business Question

A mid-size US retail chain has $2.3M in annual revenue but an overall
profit margin of only 12.47%. Leadership wants to know: where is the
business losing money, and what is the highest-leverage fix?

---

## Key Findings

| Finding | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| 8 sub-categories drive 80% of revenue | Focus sales effort on these 8 |
| Central region discounts at 20.51% avg vs East at 6.92% | Margin gap of 11 percentage points |
| Tables generate -$11,000 in total profit despite high revenue | Being sold below cost |
| Discounts above 20% produce negative profit on average | Clear pricing discipline threshold |
| Home Office segment has highest margin at 15.52% | Most profitable, least targeted |

---

## The One Recommendation

Cap discounts at 15% in the Central region with manager approval
required for exceptions. Estimated profit recovery: $40,000 to
$60,000 annually. This is 14 to 21% of total current profit,
from one process change.
